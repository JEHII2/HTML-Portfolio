The Best Movies According to Joe

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Aliens (1986) Sci-fi/Action

This is the best alien movie out of the franchise.

2. Star Wars - Return of the Jedi (1983) Sci-fi/Action

Out of the original movies (episodes 4, 5, & 6) this one was the best.

3. Black Panther (2018) Action/Adventure

One of the best Marvel movies made.
